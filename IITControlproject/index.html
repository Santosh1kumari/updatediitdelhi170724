Welcome to Control Group

The Control Group is associated with the Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. The primary focus of this group is research and teaching. We teach undergraduate and post-graduate level courses, in addition to the research-oriented control theoretic courses. Our faculty members are actively involved in state-of-the-art research work in diversified fields, such as: networked systems and control, distributed control and optimization, graph theoretic control, learning based control, robotics, adaptive and predictive control, robust and optimal control, model-order reduction, biological systems and control. In addition to the theoretical research, our group is also involved in application-oriented and industry-driven research works. The research grants that we receive from several research organizations and industries, such as: SERB, DRDO, DST, ISRO, BARC and Indian Railways, provide us unique platforms to apply our innovated control algorithms/methodologies in solving real-world problems.

Our post-graduate programs: M. Tech. and M. S. (Research) are designed to provide students advanced education and training in the field of control engineering that are complementary to the training they have acquired during their undergraduate program. The typical training is on the analysis of dynamical and complex systems, control system design and optimization, and their applications in real-world scenarios. By getting training through our specialized courses, and hands-on practice through our laboratory/practical modules, the graduates acquire sufficient technical as well as research-oriented skills to get professional positions in industries and research scholar positions at the national and international level universities. Our graduate students are placed in industries such as: Bajaj, GE, SAIL, NTPC, Amazon, John Deere,Intel,BEL, DRDO and ISRO. Some of our graduates are associated with academia. We believe that our programs provide an excellent avenue for the students in upscaling their technological knowledge, and build-up research aptitude in control engineering.
